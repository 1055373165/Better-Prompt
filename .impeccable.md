# Design Context

Status: provisional defaults for the current `better-prompt` redesign. Update this file when the product direction is refined.

## Target Audience

- Knowledge workers, product builders, developers, and analysts who collaborate with large language models frequently
- Users who can describe an intent but do not always know how to express it as a strong prompt structure

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- Turn a vague request or rough prompt into a usable, high-quality prompt artifact
- Debug prompts that produce unstable, shallow, or off-target outputs
- Evaluate prompts or outputs before reusing them in real work

## Brand Personality

- Calm
- Precise
- Professional
- Editorial rather than futuristic
- Confident without showing off
- Distinct from generic AI glow-and-gradient tooling

## Product Promise

The interface should make users feel that the system can take a half-formed thought and turn it into something rigorous, usable, and ready for work.

## Visual Direction

- Light-first workspace
- Tinted paper-like neutrals with dark ink contrast
- One assertive accent family used with discipline
- Editorial hierarchy with a more expressive display voice and a quieter product body voice
- Motion used for sequencing and clarity, not spectacle
